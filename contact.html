How To Reach us ?

By Taxi

For those coming from within Pune or nearby areas, hiring a taxi directly to Sanjay Nursery is a convenient option. Most taxi services offer fixed rates or metered fares from different parts of the city.

Approximate Cost: The cost varies based on your starting location. From central Pune, it might cost around ₹300 to ₹500.

Travel Time: Again, depending on your location, travel time can range from 20 minutes to 1 hour.

By Train

The nearest railway station is Pune Junction Railway Station.

Taxi: A taxi from Pune Junction Railway Station to Sanjay Nursery can take about 30 to 45 minutes depending on traffic, covering a distance of around 15 kilometers. The estimated fare is ₹400 to ₹600.

Public Transport: Public buses and auto-rickshaws are also available from the railway station to Loni Kalbhor. Buses might require transfers and can take longer, but are a cost-effective option..

By Air-Line

The nearest airport to Sanjay Nursery is Pune International Airport. Upon arrival, visitors have multiple options to reach the nursery.

Taxi: You can hire a taxi directly from the airport to Sanjay Nursery. The distance is approximately 20 kilometers, and it might take around 45 minutes to 1 hour depending on traffic. The cost is estimated to be around ₹600 to ₹800.

Public Transport: Public buses are available from the airport to various parts of Pune. However, you may need to change buses to reach Loni Kalbhor. This option is the most economical but also the most time-consuming. .

By Public Transport

Pune has a network of public buses operated by PMPML (Pune Mahanagar Parivahan Mahamandal Ltd) that connect various parts of the city to Loni Kalbhor.

You will have to take multiple buses to reach Loni Kalbhor. First get down at Hadapsar, Gadital and then from there you can either take another bus or a shared auto to Kadam Wak Wasti, Loni Kalbhor. Nearest Landmarks: Opp Riverview City Loni Kalbhor

Opp Manali Resorts Loni Kalbhor. From Swargate, Pune Station, Shivajinagar and other areas Cost: Fares are nominal, usually Rs.30-Rs.100 per personal Travel Time: Depending on your starting point and traffic, it can take anywhere from 1 hour to 2 hours..
